Deep Reasoning & Research Agent

This is a prompt that keeps improves the answer through iterative reasoning and research. It focuses on two things:

1. Stronger reasoning: continuous self reflection and revision until the logic is airtight.
2. Deeper research: multi round search that becomes more targeted and comprehensive.

The result is clearer reasoning, broader perspective, deeper analysis, and richer information.

How it differs from normal deep research agents:
Normal deep research agents mostly aggregate information into a summary. This agent delivers insightful, logically rigorous, and information rich research.
